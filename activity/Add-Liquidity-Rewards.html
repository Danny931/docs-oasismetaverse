Add Liquidity Rewards

 

 

OasisMetaverse (https://app.oasiswap.finance) invites you to join the OasisMetaverse ecosystem projects.

You will be rewarded for completing the following tasks:

(Attention: You can choose all or one of them without any restrictions. You can also choose to provide liquidity for multiple tokens to earn more ost rewards and airdrops!)

 

Task1 and Rewards+Airdrops: Create a token and add a liquidity pool

  • Create a token and add a liquidity pool, and you will receive 25% of the OST reward and 25% of the OST airdrop corresponding to the total value of the liquidity pool. The higher the total value of the liquidity pool, the higher the reward for OST tokens.

    Snapshot time: On the 15th day of adding the flow pool, the snapshot is automatically completed by the smart contract!

    Claim rewards/airdrops: Smart contract automatically transfers funds to your wallet immediately！

  • Rewards:

    OST rewards: 50% OST corresponding to the total value of the liquidity pool.

  • Tutorials:

    How to creat a BSC token? Click Here: Create Token

    How to add token liquidity? Click Here: Add Liquidity

 

Task2 and Rewards+Airdrops: Add Liquidity For OST/BNB or OST/USDT

  • Add Liquidity For OST/BNB or OST/USDT, and you will receive 50% of the OST reward and 50% of the OST airdrop corresponding to the total value of the liquidity pool. The higher the total value of the liquidity pool, the higher the reward for OST tokens.

    Snapshot time: On the 15th day of adding the flow pool, the snapshot is automatically completed by the smart contract!

    Claim rewards/airdrops: Smart contract automatically transfers funds to your wallet immediately！

  • Rewards:

    OST rewards: 100% OST corresponding to the total value of the liquidity pool.

  • Tutorials:

    How to add OST/BNB or OST/USDT liquidity? Click Here: Add OST Liquidity

 

Task3 and Rewards+Airdrops: Join OasisMetaverse Community

  • Join OasisMetaverse telegram group (https://t.me/OasisMetaverse_All_Users)

    Follow OasisMetaverse twitter (https://twitter.com/Oasis_Meta_BSC)

 

 

Event Period: September 22, 2021 20:00 ~ October 6, 2021 20:00 (HKT)

For more details, please see OasisMetaverse official announcement.
