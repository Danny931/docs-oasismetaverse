How to Swap

How to swap

 

Start Swap Now.

Go to the OASISwap page. Clike Here: https://app.oasiswap.finance

Copy Oasiswap Hosted list link:

https://token.oasiswap.finance/oasiswap-hosted-list.json

Hosted list Can help you avoid being deceived by the token of malicious contract code!

Then paste to "Add list"

 

 

Find the two tokens you want to trade (you can also search for the pass name or paste the pass contract address to find it).

Enter the number of transactions and click on Swap.

In the MetaMask pop-up window, click Confirm.

Wait for the transaction to complete and the token is redeemed successfully.
