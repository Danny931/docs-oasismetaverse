How to Add/Remove Liquidity

 

Add Liquidity

Go to the Pool page and click Add Liquidity.

 Enter the two passes you want to add liquidity to, enter the corresponding quantity, and click Add.

Click Confirm Supply.

Click Confirm in the MetaMask Wallet window.

Wait for the liquidity to be added after the delivery contract interaction.

Remove Liquidity

Click Manage to the right of the mobility you want to remove and expand the details.

Click Remove.

Enter the number to be removed and click Approve & Remove.

Click Confirm in the MetaMask Wallet window.

Wait for the contract to interact before liquidity removal is complete.

 

Congratulations!

Anyone that provide Liquidity for OST/USDT or OST/BNB will receive some OST token Airdrop&Rewards!
