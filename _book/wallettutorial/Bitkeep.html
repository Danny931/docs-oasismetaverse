Bitkeep

 

1. Download BitKeep Wallet

Visit BitKeep wallet official website and download App. (Address:https://bitkeep.org)

Open the bitkeep wallet after installation.

2. Connect Wallet

You can select to create wallet or import existed wallet. Take ‘New Wallet’ as an example. Select New Wallet，choose the right chain, check BSC, click Next step.

Back up mnemonics and verify mnemonics as required.

After verification, the wallet is created successfully.

3. Start the Journey with Oasiswap

On the DApp page, click the search box at the top of the page and enter the KSwap : https://app.oasiswap.finance and search.

Select Binance Smart Chain.

When you open it for the first time, a risk statement will pop up. After reading it, select Confirm and you can start with Oasiswap.
