MetaMask

MetaMask

 

1. Install MetaMask Wallet From Chrome.

MetaMask website: https://metamask.io​

In Chrome, for example, download and install the MetaMask Wallet plugin in the Chrome online app store.

Download address: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/metamask/nkbihfbeogaeaoehlefnkodbefgpgknn​

Once installed, open MetaMask.

By default, the wallet is connected to the Ethereum Mainnet, click on the Ethereum Mainnet, and in the drop-down menu, click on Custom RPC:

Fill in the BSC Mainnet RPC data in the form:

 
Mainnet RPC
 

Networt Name: BSC Mainnet

New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed1.binance.org/​

Chain ID: 56

Currency Symbol: BNB

Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com/​

Once the configuration is complete and saved, switch to that network.

2. Use Oasiswap

Take Swap for example, open the Oasiswap official website: https://app.oasiswap.finance.

Click Connect to a wallet in the upper right corner and select MetaMask.

Once connected, you can use Oasiswap.
